# BMC

The BMC have a lot of resources for supporting [affiliated clubs][club_support].

## Webinars

The BMC run [webinars][webinars] that are "_designed for the officers, leaders and volunteers within registered clubs._"
Forthcoming sessions are listed along with links to recordings of past sessions.

## Drop-in Sessions

The BMC run [drop-in sessions][drop_in] every two months for club committee members to "_join us to chat about topics or
ask questions that are important to you and the club you help to run_". Unlike the webinars these are not recorded.

[club_support]: https://www.thebmc.co.uk/en/club-support
[drop_in]: https://www.thebmc.co.uk/en/club-drop-in-sessions
[webinars]: https://www.thebmc.co.uk/en/club-support-webinar-programme
